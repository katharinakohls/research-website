Theses and Research Internships


Offered: Technical Topics

  • Evaluate the use of TTCN3 for security testing in 5G networks, Master Thesis
  • Building and evaluate ASN1 fuzzing approaches, Master Thesis

  • Implementation and performance evaluation of SUCI encryption in a memory safe language, Bachelor/Master Thesis
  • Analyzing open-source 5G core networks on TLS vulnerabilities, Bachelor/Master Thesis
  • Designing, implementing, and setting up an interconnection honey pot, Master Thesis
  • Implementation of a Signal Strength Measurement App, Bachelor Thesis, Internship
    Implement an Android app that allows to measure the signal strength of surrounding mobile network base stations. The thesis includes the design and implementation of the app as well as real-world measurements of signal strengths in an example area.
  • End-to-End Traffic Analysis Attacks in Mobile Networks, Bachelor Thesis
    Design and apply end-to-end traffic analysis attacks on 5G mobile traffic. The thesis focuses on the experimental evaluation of statistical attacks and their feasibility.
  • Remote SIM Implementation, Master Thesis
    Integrate a remote SIM setup into SRSRAN that allows to switch between multiple physical SIM cards within the software stack implementation of SRSRAN. Verify the correct behavior and conduct measurements in this setup.
